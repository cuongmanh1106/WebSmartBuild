1.BIM Concept

Durkduct system introduce BIM concept into its design, all duct segments and components are designed, produced and installed in modularized and standardized way while ensuring the accurate information transfer during each process. The efficiency of entire process are improved and material consumption is cut by least 30%.

6. Therma insulation performance

Introducing patented hot fusion composite technology, Durkduct system uses Durkflex high-quality elastomeric composite thermal insulation to composite with non-flammable air duct layer. The integrated Durkduct system has infinite mointure resistance factor, the effectively prevent the duct from water vapor permeation. Superior thermal insulation performance significantly reduce the heating or cooling loss in ari transmission.

2. Quick & Easy Installation.

Durkduct system are entirely pre fabricated in factory, it is not necessary to process on site, all segments and components are conneted by zippers and installed by cables. All finished product are folded for delivery, reducing the delivery cost. The installation time is only 1/10 of that of traditional ductwork, it takes much less time to install while improve the project quality and reduce the overall cost.

7. Fire safety

Air duct layer of Durkduct is made of non-flammable material, comforming to fire-safety requirement of public and high-rise building. The thermal insulation is made of fire-retardant Durkflex elastomeric thermal insulation material. (FM approved & Class 0)

3. Green

Durkduct system is made of green material, there is low carbon emission in production, istallation and system operation. Durkduct system earns extra credits for green project.

8. Pressure resistance

Rectangle duckwork often gets defromed by share stree. Durckduct system is designed in round or ellipse shape, converting internal stress into wall transverse tensile strength. The pressure resistance of Durkduct system (3000Pa-5000Pa) far outperforms the traditional ductwork (<= 2000Pa).

4. Air transmission performance

Durkduct are designed in round or ellipse shape. Compared with rectangle duct, the inner duct layer is more smooth, there is no eddy zone. The matetial of inner duct layer is smooth with less resistance, there is less airflow and pressure loss, effectively reduce the energy consumption.

9. Quiet operation

Durckduct systems is made of flexible material, it will never generate or transmit mechanical resonance or noise, the elastomeric thermal insulation layer with microcellular structure could effectively absorb and reduce the noise. Matching with sound absorbing plenum, Durkduct system operates more quietly.

5. Air leakage

Traditional ductwork are connected by flanges, air leakage exsits universally because of untight sealing of the joints. Durkduct system introduces Bidirectional Self-locking Throttling Sealing Technology (BSTS), matching with YKK double-sealing heavy-duty zipper, the air leakage is reduced by 70%.

10. Cost saving

Durkduct is designed in round and ellipse shape. Compare with rectangle duct, the resistance and noise is smaller, the inlet air velocity can be designed at higher level, the air supply volume in unit area is larger, smaller diameter can be selected, the material consumption can be cut by at least 30%.
